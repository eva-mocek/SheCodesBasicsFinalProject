Juniper & Eve

Sustainable. Gender-free. Underwear.

Small-batch, sustainable underwear made for everyone across the gender spectrum.

Sustainable.
We live in an age of fast fashion, where clothing is made, worn and thrown away in the blink of an eye. At Juniper & Eve, we want to change that. All our products are made one by one, with high quality, earth-friendly fabrics, in our studio. And our prices reflect the fair wages paid to our team.

Inclusive.
We are committed to providing a safe, welcoming experience for everyone across the gender spectrum, and are an LGBTQ+ safe space. No matter who you are, you are welcome here. Period.

Creative.
A fashion designer is, first and foremost, an artist. Clothing can play with the way we feel, how we see ourselves, and affects how we connect with the world around us. At Juniper & Eve, our designs and colours seek to capture the feelings of inner peace, comfort in our own skin, and meaningful connection with nature.

Small Business.
And we mean really small. Currently, outside of the amazing photographers, product testers and models we work with, Juniper & Eve is a one woman operation. So when we say ‘made by hand’, you know exactly which two hands we’re talking about!
